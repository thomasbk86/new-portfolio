ABOUT

I'm a UX/UI designer focused on connecting the user experience with beautiful and intuitive interface design. I believe we're entering an exciting time with a unique opportunity to positively change lives through design and technology. I hope to be a part of that change.

MY STORY

When I was a boy, my grandma and I would listen to Frank Sinatra, Dean Martin and Elvis Presley on the radio. One day I found a book full of cartoon versions of famous musicians and looked up my grandma’s favorite artists. My first memories of drawing are copying those caricatures and giving them to her as a gift. That’s when I knew I wanted to create things for others to enjoy. The long and winding road to UX has been a natural progression stemming from that realization.


My creative interests have always been pulled in many directions throughout my life. I moved on from drawing caricatures to studying Michelangelo sketches in my free time and taking up woodworking in high school. I also joined the film class and began producing shorts for the school, which lead me to the New York Film academy to develop skills in writing, producing and filming. I also began teaching myself the piano and writing songs as another outlet for creative energy.

I was told to be practical though and chose the Strategic Communication major at the Ohio State University to prepare myself for the real world. After graduation, I had a few short stints at startups, spent a couple of years as a medical recruiter and then landed at a leading technology advisory firm. While these positions challenged me, I was always trying to find a career where I could create things for others to enjoy.

CAREER CHANGE

A friend of mine had been going through the same challenges and decided to learn coding, which allowed him to work remotely and travel whenever he wanted. He found a way to leave the typical job structure behind and pursue a passion, which inspired me. As he began developing applications, he asked for design help, which introduced me to the world of UX and UI. Finally there was a profession I thought could utilize my creative energy in new and exciting ways.

Through the encouragement of family, friends, and my wife, I left the technology advisory firm and began the UX/UI designer course with Bloc. The program combined design fundamentals, prototyping skills, frontend web development programming skills, and a mentorship from a senior designer.

Now that I’ve completed the program, I can’t wait to get started creating user experiences and interfaces to help change the way people interact with mobile devices and the web. Making a career change has been challenging but I’m so excited to see where the future leads in UX and UI design. Please take a look at my project case studies for a more detailed look into my design process and feel free to contact me with any questions.
